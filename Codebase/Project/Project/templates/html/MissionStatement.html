Mission Statement

Our mission is to use satellite-based monitoring to identify northern Canadian communities at risk from permafrost thaw and coastal erosion. By combining geospatial data with climate indicators, we aim to help protect vulnerable coastlines, inform infrastructure planning, and support long-term environmental resilience.
